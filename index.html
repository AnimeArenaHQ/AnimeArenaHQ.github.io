▶ See the integration demo How it works

The flow

Three steps, one account — ours.

01 · LOGIN KIT

The owner signs in

The account owner authorizes AnimeArenaHQ through TikTok OAuth. We confirm which account we're connected to — nothing more.

02 · CONTENT POSTING API

We upload our video

A finished vertical video we produced is sent to TikTok with push_by_file (FILE_UPLOAD) — direct post or draft.

03 · PUBLISHED

It lands on our profile

The post completes on @animearena65. We read back only the publish status of the video we just posted.

Least privilege

The permissions we request, and why.

  • user.info.basic Confirm the authorized account. We read the connected account's open id, display name, and avatar so we know we're publishing to the right profile.
  • video.publish Direct-post our finished videos. Publishes a completed video we created straight to our own TikTok profile.
  • video.upload Send drafts. Uploads a video as a draft to our own account so it can be reviewed before going live.
Scope of use. This is a first-party tool. It publishes only videos AnimeArenaHQ produces, only to our own authorized TikTok account. We do not post on behalf of anyone else, and we do not collect, store, or process the personal data of viewers or other TikTok users. Referenced anime characters and franchises remain the property of their rights holders; our videos are commentary and analysis.
