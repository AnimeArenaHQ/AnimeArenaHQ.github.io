Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026 · AnimeArenaHQ

AnimeArenaHQ ("we", "us", "our") operates the AnimeArenaHQ content channels and the internal tooling we use to create and publish short-form and long-form anime commentary videos. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle and how.

1. Who we are

AnimeArenaHQ is an independent faceless content brand publishing "who would win" anime matchup videos on platforms including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. Contact: GoldGoldFruit21@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Our publishing tool connects to our own social accounts to upload videos we produce. Through the TikTok API we access only the minimum needed to publish content to our own authorized account: the account's basic profile info (open id, display name, avatar) and the permission to post videos. We do not collect, store, or process the personal data of any other TikTok users or of our viewers.

3. How we use information

  • To authenticate our own social accounts.
  • To publish videos we created to those accounts.
  • To read back the status of a post we made.

We do not sell data, run advertising profiles, or share information with third parties for marketing.

4. Data storage & retention

Access tokens for our own accounts are stored privately in our local configuration and are used only to publish our content. We retain them only as long as the integration is active and revoke them when it is not.

5. Third-party platforms

When we publish to TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram, your interaction with that content is governed by that platform's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6. Children

Our content and tooling are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data from viewers or third-party users, there is generally no personal data of yours for us to access or delete. For any privacy question, email GoldGoldFruit21@gmail.com.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the current version.
